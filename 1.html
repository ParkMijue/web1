What is Corona19?

Even if Corona's time has passed perfectly, our lives will not be the same. We call it the 'Post Corona'.

This is because there is no law that never knows when, where, and where the virus, which has an infectious virus, will shake the world. With Corona 19, we're more susceptible to disease than ever before, reluctant to take off masks with strangers, and make the most of Internet technology than ever before.
